Section 1
The Application

TeamTracker – Sports Calendar ("Licensed Application") is a piece of software created to provide sports fans with a centralized platform to track their favorite teams, view game schedules, and manage team calendars in one simple, easy-to-use app — and customized for iOS and Android mobile devices ("Devices"). It is used for users to search for sports teams, follow multiple teams, and access an aggregated calendar showing upcoming games and events for all followed teams.
